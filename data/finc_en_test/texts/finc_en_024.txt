Free FT event: Locking down the right investment strategy
Private investors can register to attend webinar with investment experts

	This year has been a turbulent one for private investors — and it’s not over yet. To help lock down your future investment strategy, readers of FT Money can register for a free place at our next Digital Dialogues webinar.

To be held at 12 noon UK time on Tuesday October 13, consumer editor Claer Barrett will chair a panel of investment experts giving their views on the outlook and guidance on how to plan, allocate and invest in turbulent times.

Speakers include Ian McCafferty, former member of the Bank of England’s Monetary Policy Committee; Jenny Davis, investment manager at Baillie Gifford, and Nick Fletcher, senior partner at London Wall Partners, which is sponsoring the event.
